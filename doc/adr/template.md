# NNNN. Short title of the decision

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the issue we're seeing that motivates this decision? Describe
the forces at play — technical constraints, the Saturn hardware reality,
project goals, prior ADRs. State facts, not opinions. This section should
let a reader understand *why a decision was unavoidable* without already
knowing the outcome.

## Decision

What we will do, in active voice: "We will …". Be specific and concrete.
If the decision establishes a rule or invariant, state it sharply enough
that a reviewer can point at a diff and say "that violates ADR-NNNN".

## Consequences

What becomes easier, and what becomes harder, as a result. Include the
costs we are knowingly accepting — an honest ADR names the downsides. Note
any follow-up work the decision implies.

## Alternatives considered

Each option we weighed and the reason it lost. "None" is rarely the
honest answer; if a decision was truly forced, say what the only other
path would have cost.
